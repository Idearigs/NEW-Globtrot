Honeymoon Tours 4.8

Experience the charm of Sri Lanka’s beautiful beaches, cultural landmarks

Indulge in a dreamy honeymoon with our 5-day tour package in the stunning coastal paradise of Sri Lanka! Your journey begins in Bentota, where sun-kissed beaches and serene landscapes set the perfect romantic backdrop. Relax and unwind at your beachfront hotel, enjoying the soothing sound of the waves and breathtaking sunsets.

On your second day, bask in leisure as you explore Bentota’s beautiful surroundings at your own pace. Visit the enchanting Madhu River, where you can take a romantic boat ride through lush mangroves, or discover the fascinating Turtle Hatchery, where you’ll witness baby turtles being released into the sea—a truly heartwarming experience!

As you continue your adventure, journey to the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site, where cobblestone streets and colonial architecture await. Stroll hand-in-hand along the fort walls and explore charming boutiques and cafes. Finally, immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of Colombo with a captivating city tour, showcasing the best of Sri Lanka's capital.

This honeymoon tour is designed for couples seeking a blend of relaxation, exploration, and romance. Create unforgettable memories together in this tropical paradise!

Highlights

Experience the charm of Sri Lanka’s beautiful beaches, cultural landmarks, and warm hospitality, ensuring a romantic getaway like no other!

  • Relaxation on Bentota’s pristine beaches
  • Scenic boat ride on the Madhu River
  • Visit the Turtle Hatchery and witness turtle releases
  • Explore the historic Galle Fort
  • City tour of Colombo with vibrant sights and sounds
