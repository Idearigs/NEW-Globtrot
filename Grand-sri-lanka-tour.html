Discover the beauty, history, and adventure of Sri Lanka on this enriching 14-day family tour

Discover the beauty, history, and adventure of Sri Lanka on this enriching 14-day family tour. From the sandy beaches of Negombo to the ancient cities of Habarana and Polonnaruwa, you’ll uncover Sri Lanka’s historical treasures. Journey through Kandy’s cultural sites, relax in the cool tea country of Nuwara Eliya, and explore the scenic wonders of Ella. Enjoy thrilling wildlife safaris in Yala, visit the colonial charm of Galle, and relax on the serene beaches of Bentota. This tour is designed for families looking for a perfect balance of leisure, adventure, and cultural immersion, leaving you with unforgettable memories.

Highlights

A 14-day family-friendly exploration of Sri Lanka’s iconic cities, natural wonders, wildlife experiences, and pristine beaches, with plenty of leisure and adventure for all ages.

  • Lagoon boat ride in Negombo
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Jeep safari in Minneriya National Park
  • Kandy’s Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Gregory’s Lake and strawberry farm in Nuwara Eliya
  • Zipline and Nine Arch Bridge in Ella
  • Jeep safari in Yala National Park
  • Galle’s Dutch Fort
  • Turtle hatchery and Madhu River safari in Bentota

Inclusions and Exclusions

  • Private airconditioned vehicle for transfers and site visits.
  • English speaking licensed chauffeur tour guide.
  • Bottled water.
