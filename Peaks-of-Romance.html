Hiking Tours 4.8

Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka's natural beauty

Embark on an unforgettable 10-day honeymoon adventure in the breathtaking island paradise of Sri Lanka! This romantic journey begins in Negombo, where you’ll relax on beautiful beaches and explore the vibrant local culture. Enjoy a leisurely city tour, soaking up the sun and savoring fresh seafood.

Your adventure continues in Sigiriya, where you’ll climb the iconic Lion Rock, surrounded by lush gardens and stunning views. Experience the thrill of a jeep safari at Minneriya National Park, where you can witness majestic elephants in their natural habitat.

Next, journey to Kandy, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and stunning botanical gardens. Engage in a cultural show showcasing the island's rich heritage. Then, travel to the cool hills of Nuwara Eliya, known for its picturesque tea plantations and charming city tours, including the beautiful Horton Plains.

As you head towards Yala, prepare for an exciting jeep safari in Yala National Park, where you can spot leopards, elephants, and other wildlife. Finally, unwind in Bentota, enjoying leisure time by the beach, visiting the Madhu River, and experiencing the local turtle hatchery.

This immersive tour is designed for couples seeking romance, adventure, and unforgettable memories in Sri Lanka’s stunning landscapes and rich culture!

Highlights

Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka's natural beauty, cultural treasures, and thrilling wildlife encounters for a honeymoon filled with unforgettable moments!
