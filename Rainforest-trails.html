Hiking Tours 4.8

Experience the best of Sri Lanka’s natural beauty and cultural treasures with thrilling hikes

Embark on a captivating 12-day hiking adventure in the enchanting landscapes of Sri Lanka! This tour is designed for those who seek to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature while exploring the rich cultural heritage of this island paradise.

Your journey begins in the coastal city of Negombo, where you can relax and enjoy a city tour. Next, you'll travel to Kandy, home to the revered Temple of the Tooth and the famous Elephant Orphanage. Experience the heartwarming interactions with these gentle giants and soak in the vibrant culture through a mesmerizing cultural show.

As you venture to Theldeniya, prepare for an exhilarating hiking experience in the stunning hills. Continue to Nuwara Eliya, where you'll explore the breathtaking Horton Plains, known for its diverse wildlife and picturesque landscapes.

You'll then make your way to Udawalawe National Park for an unforgettable jeep safari, where you can spot elephants and other magnificent wildlife in their natural habitat. The adventure continues in the lush Singharaja Rain Forest, where guided hikes will allow you to discover unique flora and fauna.

Finally, unwind in Bentota, where you can enjoy a relaxing excursion along the Madhu River and visit the Turtle Hatchery. Conclude your journey with a sense of fulfillment, having created lasting memories in this beautiful country. Join us for this incredible hiking tour that promises adventure, exploration, and a deeper connection with nature and culture in Sri Lanka!
