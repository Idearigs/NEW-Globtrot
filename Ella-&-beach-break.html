Famliy Tours 4.8

Explore the Beauty of Maldives and enjoy

This 3-day tour takes you on a fun and relaxing journey through some of Sri Lanka's most scenic and exciting locations. Start in Ella, where you'll trek to Little Adam’s Peak, explore the iconic Nine Arches Bridge, and visit the majestic Ravana Falls. Then, head to Mirissa for a thrilling whale-watching adventure, followed by visits to Galle Fort and the Kosgoda Sea Turtle Conservation. It’s the perfect family-friendly escape, combining nature, history, and unforgettable experiences!

Highlights

Discover the natural beauty and wildlife of Sri Lanka, from the mountains of Ella to the coastal adventures in Mirissa.

  • Hike Little Adam’s Peak in Ella
  • Visit Nine Arches Bridge
  • Explore Ravana Waterfall
  • Visit Buduruwagala Temple
  • Whale watching in Mirissa
  • Explore Galle Fort
  • Visit Kosgoda Turtle Conservation
  • River boat safari
