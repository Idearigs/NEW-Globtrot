Hiking Tours 4.8

Experience the best of Sri Lanka’s natural beauty and cultural treasures

Get ready for an exhilarating 12-day hiking adventure through the stunning landscapes of Sri Lanka! This tour is perfect for those who crave the great outdoors while immersing themselves in the vibrant culture of this beautiful island.

Your journey begins in Negombo, where you'll explore the charming coastal town and relax after your travels. Next, you'll travel to Kandy, home to breathtaking hills and the sacred Temple of the Tooth. Here, you’ll experience the rich culture of Sri Lanka through local hikes, stunning views, and perhaps even a cultural show.

The adventure continues as you head to Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as “Little England.” Explore the picturesque town, hike in the breathtaking Horton Plains, and trek up Mini Adams Peak for awe-inspiring views. Your next stop is Ella, famous for its stunning scenery and vibrant tea plantations. Visit the iconic Nine Arch Bridge and take in the spectacular views from Ella Rock.

Afterward, you'll embark on a thrilling jeep safari in Yala National Park, where you might spot leopards, elephants, and other incredible wildlife. Continue your journey to Bentota, where you can unwind on the beautiful beaches and explore the Madhu River and Turtle Hatchery.

Finally, wrap up your adventure with a city tour of Colombo, the bustling capital, before heading back to the airport. This hiking tour promises to be an unforgettable experience filled with breathtaking scenery, exciting wildlife encounters, and a deep connection to Sri Lanka’s rich culture. Join us for this extraordinary journey!
